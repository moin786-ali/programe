    What will I learn?
    


    In this program, you’ll learn UI design in Figma and participate
in a Google Ventures-style design sprint to get a taste of the user-centered 
design process. You’ll also be exposed to the realistic scenario of ramping up 
on a team with an existing design system. At the end of the course, you’ll be
able to think, speak, and solve problems like an industry professional.
    


     Foundation Project
    


    You'll work solo designing a mobile app based 
on a given problem statement. Every lesson will 
have an application activity that relates back to this mobile app.
    

Discovery: Research your users, conduct interviews, and gain insights.

Synthesizing: Organize your research data to set yourself up for brainstorming ideas.

Wireframing: Begin initial designs on your screens.

Patterns: Learn UX concepts that focus on user interactions within your designs.

Details: Focus on small details within each screen. 

Hierarchy: Learn how to visually prioritize your screen content and features.

Usability Testing: Test your designs in front of real users.

Foundation Conclusion: Turn your foundation project into a case study and write 
about your first KGN project.


    Design Systems Project
    

    Work individually to apply the Material Design
system to a 2-week UI design project. Get exposed 
to the realistic scenario of ramping up on a new team 
with an existing design system.
    



    Collaboration Project
    

    Your final project will be presented at the end of the KGN Design track.
