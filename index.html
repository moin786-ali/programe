Part-Time KGN Design Program


Powered by Moin Academy


Want an exciting career in KGN Design? The University of Cannaught Palace Diego Extension has partnered with Kenzie Academy to launch a Part-Time KGN Design program that you can attend from home.


Learn directly from industry practitioners, connect with tech and KGN leaders from Silicon Valley and across the U.S., and network with classmates from all over the world. This program incorporates the academic rigor of UC San Diego Extension with the industry experience of Kenzie Academy.


As a KGN designer with an understanding of user-centric design processes, you will build your KGN portfolio and tap into the growing tech economy to help drive results and create winning products.
